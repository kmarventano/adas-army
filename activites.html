About Me

I'm a student at NYU in the College of Arts and Science majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. Prior to college, I never used a computer for anything more than Netflix and writing papers. I started out in college as a math major with aspirations to go into data analysis. My degree program required a few computer science classes. I was terrified of these classes, but I ended up really enjoying them, so much so that the second semester of my junior year, I switched to being a CS major. I’m interested in going into either software development or data science.

Outside of school, my hobbies include jamming on my clarinet, skiing, hiking, and biking. I’m the proud owner of a crazy chocolate lab, Katy. I love to take her on adventures, and document her feuds with lawn furniture around the neighborhood.
